Frequently Asked Questions

Yatra Vats se judi kuch aam jaankari jo aapke kaam aayegi.

How can I book a group tour with Yatra Vats?

Booking bahut simple hai! Aap website par diye gaye WhatsApp button ya "Call Now" par click karke humse direct baat kar sakte hain. Hum aapko itinerary bhejenge aur aap token amount dekar seat book kar sakte hain.

Saurabh Patel personally tours lead karte hain?

Haan, hamare zyadaatar flagship group tours ko Saurabh Patel khud lead karte hain taaki aapko hamare YouTube vlogs jaisa hi real aur safe experience mil sake.

What is included in the tour package?

Hamare packages mein genellikle AC transport, acche hotels (Double/Triple sharing), breakfast aur dinner, aur sightseeing included hote hain. Har tour ki specific details itinerary mein di jati hai.

Is it safe for solo female travelers and families?

Bilkul! Yatra Vats ki pehchan hi hamari community hai. Hum safety par sabse zyada dhyan dete hain, isliye hamare saath solo ladies aur families bina kisi darr ke travel karti hain.
